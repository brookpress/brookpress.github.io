Brook Pressは東京の小川町（Brookは小川の意）を拠点に、2023年に始動した小規模で実験的な出版のプロジェクトです。武蔵野美術大学芸術文化学科の古賀稔章ゼミに集う学生が主体となり、印刷機リソグラフを活用して、執筆・編集・デザイン・印刷・製本・流通の全てのプロセスを自ら担って作成する雑誌『PAN』の刊行を軸に、外部機関と連携したキュラトリアルな社会実践や、専門家を招聘したレクチャー、ワークショップ等も展開。私たちが生きる世界に、印刷物を通じて小さな声をあげるための出版活動のプロトタイプを探求しています。

Brook Press is a small-scale, experimental publishing practice based in Ogawa-cho, Tokyo (Brook being a translation of 小川(Ogawa), meaning “small river”), launched in 2023. Led by students from the seminar of Toshiaki Koga at the department of Art, Culture and Design Studies, Musashino Art University, the project centers on the publication of a magazine called PAN — produced entirely in-house using a Risograph printer, with students handling all aspects of writing, editing, design, printing, binding, and distribution. Beyond the magazine, Brook Press also engages in curatorial social practice in collaboration with external institutions, as well as lectures and workshops led by invited specialists. The project explores a prototype for publishing as a way of raising small voices in the world we inhabit — through printed matter.
